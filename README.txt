AGILE99 STARTER KIT v2.1
==========================
agile99.com | github.com/joelfrenette/agile99-starter-kit

START HERE. Work through the folders in order:

  1-START-HERE/         Open START-HERE.html in your browser.
                        Pick your AI tool. Follow the setup checklist.
                        ~45 minutes to complete.

  2-COPY-TO-PROJECT/    Copy the rules files for YOUR tool into your project.
                        Always include CONTEXT.md and PROMPT-LOG.md.
                        Also include DESIGN.md and SECURITY_RULES.md.

  3-PASTE-INTO-CHAT/    Paste MORPHEUS-SYSTEM-PROMPT.md as your first message
                        in every new chat session. This starts the entire loop.

  4-REFERENCE/          Full methodology, persona prompts, audit templates.
                        Read once. Pull from when needed.

That is the whole system.
3 files in your repo + 1 paste = Agile99 running.
