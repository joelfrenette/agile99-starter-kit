STEP 3 — PASTE INTO V0 OR DYAD TO START THE LOOP
==================================================
Open MORPHEUS-SYSTEM-PROMPT.md in any text editor.

Copy everything BELOW the line of dashes (---).

Open a NEW CHAT in V0.app or Dyad.sh.
Paste it as your FIRST MESSAGE and send.

That is it. No settings. No project config.
Just paste it as your first message every new chat.

Morpheus will respond with:
"Wake up, Neo... what brings you here today?"

Then answer with a number (1-7) or just describe
what you want to do. Morpheus routes to the right
crew member automatically.

Do this at the start of every new chat session.
